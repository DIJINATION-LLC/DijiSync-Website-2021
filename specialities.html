Surgery

● RCM Challenge

Your billing staff needs to be well-versed in usage of the global surgical packages, modifiers for surgeon roles and same-day procedures, and documentation for unlisted procedures.

● Our Unique Solution

DijiSync offers expert staff with a collective Surgery related medical billing experience of more than 50 years.

Internal Medicine

● RCM Challenge

The physician may not be aware of the codes he/she can use in addition to the office level codes to achieve higher reimbursements.

● Our Unique Solution

DijiSync offers a Code Review service by certified coders (frequency could be bi-annual).

Orthopedic

● RCM Challenge

Ortho practices usually have rehab centers as a part of the practice. The practice would usually bill the office visits along with the rehab codes without correct modifiers which causes a high denial rate.

● Our Unique Solution

DijiSync EMR highlights the old codes with a different color (Red), which indicates the provider to use a different code in order to be reimbursed.

Cardiology

● RCM Challenge

Billing staff must have a thorough understanding of the appropriate codes and modifiers required for cardiology procedures like pacemakers, different types of percutaneous coronary interventions, and peripheral vascular procedures.

● Our Unique Solution

DijiSync offers expert staff with a collective Cardiology related medical billing experience of more than 50 years.

Gastroenterology

● RCM Challenge

As a gastroenterologist, the provider works with patients who have a wide range of healthcare needs, from consultative visits to procedures requiring anesthesia like colonoscopies and surgery. As a result, billing can become complex, particularly when dealing with issues like underpayments or claim denials.

● Our Unique Solution

DijiSync deploys medical billers and coders that have the most indepth experience in working with Gastroentrologists ensuring that all the correct procedures are followed in order to maintain and increase the reimbursements.
